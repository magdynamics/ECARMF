# Chapter 4 — Economic Digital Cell and Economic Digital Asset

## Economic Digital Cell Definition
An Economic Digital Cell (EDC) is the smallest independently identifiable, governable, measurable, and traceable unit of economic value within the ECARMF ecosystem.

## Economic Digital Asset Definition
An Economic Digital Asset (EDA) is a managed economic asset composed of one or more Economic Digital Cells.

## Requirement EDC-0001
Every ECARMF Entity shall be decomposable into one or more Economic Digital Cells where applicable.

## Requirement EDC-0002
Every Economic Digital Cell shall inherit from the Universal Base Entity.

## Requirement EDC-0003
Every Economic Digital Cell shall maintain independent lifecycle management.

## Requirement EDC-0004
Every Economic Digital Cell shall participate in the Knowledge Graph.

## Requirement EDC-0005
Every Economic Digital Cell shall be independently measurable.

## Requirement EDA-0001
Every Economic Digital Asset shall be composed of one or more Economic Digital Cells.
