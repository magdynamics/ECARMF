# Chapter 7 — Universal Attribute Library

## Requirement UAL-0001
Every Canonical Entity shall inherit the Universal Attribute Library.

## Attribute Domains

1. Identity
2. Classification
3. Ownership
4. Lifecycle
5. Economic
6. Risk
7. Operational
8. Governance
9. Relationship
10. Evidence
11. Security
12. Metadata

## Requirement IDN-0001
Every Entity shall possess one immutable Universal Identifier.

## Requirement OWN-0001
Every Entity shall define one accountable owner.

## Requirement LIF-0001
Every Entity shall maintain complete lifecycle history.

## Requirement REL-0001
Every Entity shall participate within the ECARMF Knowledge Graph.
