# Chapter 5 — Universal Laws

## UNI-0001 Universal Identity
Every Economic Digital Cell shall possess a globally unique immutable identity.

## UNI-0002 Universal Value
Every EDC shall represent measurable economic value, economic obligation, or economic influence.

## UNI-0003 Universal Relationship
No EDC exists in isolation; every EDC shall participate in one or more governed relationships.

## UNI-0004 Universal Context
The meaning of every EDC is determined by context.

## UNI-0005 Universal Time
Every EDC exists through time and every state transition shall be preserved.

## UNI-0006 Universal Evidence
Every analytical conclusion shall be supported by verifiable evidence.

## UNI-0007 Universal Intelligence
Knowledge shall be transformed into decisions through the Layered Intelligence Architecture.

## UNI-0008 Universal Explainability
Every recommendation shall be explainable through complete traceability.

## UNI-0009 Universal Optimization
Every decision shall seek to maximize sustainable economic value while respecting governance, capital constraints, acceptable risk, and stakeholder objectives.

## UNI-0010 Universal Learning
Every completed decision shall improve future reasoning.
