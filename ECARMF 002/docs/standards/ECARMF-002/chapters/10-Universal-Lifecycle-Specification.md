# Chapter 10 — Universal Lifecycle Specification

## Requirement ULS-0001
Every Canonical Entity shall implement the Universal Lifecycle.

## Lifecycle

Concept → Draft → Proposed → Validated → Approved → Active → Operational → Modified → Suspended → Retired → Archived

## Requirement LIF-3001
Every Entity shall begin in Draft state.

## Requirement LIF-3002
Activation requires successful validation.

## Requirement LIF-3003
Operational status requires formal approval.

## Requirement LIF-3004
Archived entities shall remain permanently accessible for audit.
