# Chapter 15 — Conformance, Certification & Release

## Requirement CON-0001
Only implementations satisfying all mandatory ECARMF requirements may claim ECARMF compliance.

## Compliance Levels

1. Foundation Compliance
2. Ontology Compliance
3. Intelligence Compliance
4. Enterprise Compliance

## Requirement CON-0010
Compliance shall be cumulative.

## Requirement TRC-0001
One hundred percent requirement traceability is mandatory.

## Requirement REL-0001
A released version shall be immutable.
