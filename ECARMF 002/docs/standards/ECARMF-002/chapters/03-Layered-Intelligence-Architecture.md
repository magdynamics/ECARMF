# Chapter 3 — Layered Intelligence Architecture

## Requirement LIA-0001
Every analytical process shall begin with validated data.

## Requirement LIA-0002
Information shall be derived only from validated data.

## Requirement LIA-0003
Knowledge shall be represented through the ECARMF Knowledge Graph.

## Requirement LIA-0004
Understanding shall identify causal relationships.

## Requirement LIA-0005
Intelligence shall evaluate multiple decision alternatives.

## Requirement LIA-0006
Wisdom shall optimize long-term economic value rather than short-term metrics alone.

## Requirement LIA-0007
Learning shall continuously improve analytical models based on observed outcomes.
