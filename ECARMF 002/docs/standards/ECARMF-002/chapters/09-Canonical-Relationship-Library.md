# Chapter 9 — Canonical Relationship Library

## Requirement CRL-0001
Every relationship shall be instantiated as a Relationship Entity.

## Relationship Domains

- Ownership: OWNS, OPERATES, MANAGES, LEASES, CONTROLS
- Financial: FUNDS, FINANCES, INVESTS_IN, GUARANTEES, SECURES
- Risk: EXPOSED_TO, MITIGATES, TRANSFERS, REDUCES, AMPLIFIES
- Operational: USES, CONSUMES, PRODUCES, MAINTAINS, DEPENDS_ON
- Governance: APPROVES, AUDITS, CERTIFIES, VALIDATES, REQUIRES
- Analytical: FORECASTS, SIMULATES, OPTIMIZES, EVALUATES, SCORES
- Technical: IMPLEMENTS, EXTENDS, INHERITS, CALLS, STORES
- Temporal: PRECEDES, SUCCEEDS, REPLACES, VALID_DURING
- Influence: AFFECTS, INFLUENCES, IMPACTS, CONSTRAINS, ENABLES

## Requirement CAR-3001
Every relationship shall explicitly define cardinality.
