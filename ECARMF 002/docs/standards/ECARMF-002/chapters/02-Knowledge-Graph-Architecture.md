# Chapter 2 — Knowledge Graph Architecture

## Requirement KG-0001
Every ECARMF implementation shall maintain a logical knowledge graph representing all managed entities.

## Requirement KG-0002
Every Entity shall be represented by exactly one canonical graph node.

## Requirement KG-0003
Every Relationship shall be represented by a Relationship Entity.

## Requirement KG-0004
Every graph node and graph relationship shall maintain lifecycle history.

## Temporal Requirement
Historical graph states shall remain queryable and reproducible.
