# ECARMF-004 — Asset Intelligence Model

## Engineering Construction Specification

### 1. Standard Purpose

ECARMF-004 establishes the mandatory construction requirements for implementing Asset Intelligence in the ECARMF platform.

Where:

- ECARMF-001 defines the framework principles;
- ECARMF-002 defines the economic ontology and meta model;
- ECARMF-003 defines the reference architecture;

ECARMF-004 defines how assets become intelligent, governed, measurable, traceable, and actionable digital economic systems.

### 2. Scope

This standard applies to every ECARMF implementation that manages, evaluates, monitors, simulates, or reasons about economic assets.

It defines engineering requirements for:

- Asset registration
- Asset decomposition
- EDA construction
- EDC construction
- Digital Twin generation
- Asset state management
- Asset evidence management
- Asset performance intelligence
- Asset risk and capital linkage
- Asset AI reasoning
- Developer and AI coding agent implementation rules
