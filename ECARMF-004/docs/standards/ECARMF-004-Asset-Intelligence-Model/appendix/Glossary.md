# ECARMF-004 Glossary

## Asset Intelligence

The capability to transform asset data into governed, explainable, and actionable economic knowledge.

## Economic Digital Asset

A managed economic asset composed of one or more Economic Digital Cells.

## Economic Digital Cell

The smallest independently identifiable, governable, measurable, and traceable unit of economic value.

## Asset Digital Twin

A governed digital representation of an Economic Digital Asset across state, time, evidence, performance, risk, capital, and decisions.

## Asset Context

The complete set of economic, operational, risk, capital, evidence, and lifecycle information required to evaluate an asset.
