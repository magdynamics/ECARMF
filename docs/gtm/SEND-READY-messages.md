## SEQUENCE (how a prospect moves)

1. **Touch 1 — the offer** (email or, for JJ Fish, a call). Ask: "want the brief?"
2. **Touch 2 — deliver** the branded PDF the moment they say yes.
3. **Touch 3 — the 20-minute demo** on a tenant shaped like their business.
4. **Close** — founding-client terms, key issued on the call.

## The three rules for every send

1. **Give first, ask microscopically.** The only ask in touch 1 is "want the brief?" — a yes
   is nearly free for them and it starts everything.
2. **Their facts, never our features.** Touch 1 mentions the platform in one sentence at most.
   The product enters at the demo, opening on a tenant shaped like *them*.
3. **Verify before send, remove the internal note.** One wrong fact (wrong owner, wrong site)
   ends the credibility. MAG confirms ownership; the red INTERNAL line comes off the PDF.
