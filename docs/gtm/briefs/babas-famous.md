## The pattern that matters
Three of four failures are the **identical root cause: no certified/trained manager on site.**
That is a *credential-tracking* failure — someone's certificate lapsed, or a new site opened
without one, and no system flagged it. It repeats across your locations because each site
tracks (or forgets) its own paperwork in isolation. And notice the structure: at least one
location is separately incorporated ("…ON LARAMIE, INC") — you already run as **multiple legal
entities**, which is exactly the shape our platform is built around.
