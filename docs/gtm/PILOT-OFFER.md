# The Founding Client Offer

*Hand this to a warm account on the first call. One page, no fine print energy.*

## What you get, live from day one

- **Your whole business in one governed brain** — every location and legal entity, its bank
  and application feeds, its risks scored and mapped, its compliance posture computed daily.
- **A board pack that writes itself** — whole-company or per-location, printable, current.
- **An AI analyst on your premises** — ask it anything about your numbers; it answers from
  *your* data, cites what it looked at, and your information never leaves your building.
- **Alarms instead of surprises** — state your expectations ("GP% ≥ 25%", "no movement over
  $10k", "zero compliance slips") and the system watches every record, every day.
- **Every original document preserved** — bank files, statements, uploads: hash-sealed,
  searchable, attributable. Audit-ready by construction.

## The founding-client deal, plainly

You get the platform at half price for two years and a product team that treats your requests
as next week's features. We get the two things money can't buy: your real-world proof and your
name as a founding client (with your written approval, only ever with your approval).

## The promise

30 days. If the risk board, the board pack, and the AI analyst haven't earned their fee,
walk away — your data exports with you, and the original documents were always yours.

## The three sentences that answer the three fears

- *"Is my data safe?"* — The AI runs on our server, not the cloud; regulated tenants require
  keys; every access is logged with a name attached.
- *"Will AI make mistakes with my business?"* — The AI recommends; a human decides. The
  software enforces it — an unapproved action is refused by the platform itself.
- *"What if you disappear?"* — Your data exports completely, and every backup is
  restore-verified nightly. We prove it, we don't promise it.
