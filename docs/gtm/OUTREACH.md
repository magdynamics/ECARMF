# Outreach Scripts — warm first, then cold

*Director-drafted, human-sent. Every message is short enough to read on a phone.*

---

## 1. The warm call (JJ Fish, Tenant-10, and friends) — say it, don't send it

> "You know that platform I've been building? I have to show you something — **your business
> is already inside it.** Your locations, your numbers, your risks — live, on a screen, scored
> daily. Twenty minutes, I'll show you your own dashboard. If it's not the most useful twenty
> minutes of your week, lunch is on me."

On the call: open THEIR tenant, show the risk board → the board pack → ask the AI analyst one
question about their real numbers. Then slide PILOT-OFFER across the table. Issue their access
key while they watch — possession closes.

## Objection cheat-sheet (director keeps this current)

| They say | We say |
|---|---|
| "We have QuickBooks / a PMS already" | "Keep them. We're the layer above — they feed us; we watch, score, and explain. Nothing gets replaced, everything gets supervised." |
| "AI scares our compliance officer" | "That's exactly who we built it for — bring them to the demo. Ask them what they think of software where the AI *cannot* act without a named human's approval and every answer carries its evidence." |
| "We're too small for this" | "You're too small NOT to have it — you carry enterprise risks without an enterprise risk department. That's the entire point of the price." |
| "How do we know it works?" | "Because you'll watch your own data in it before you pay a dollar. The 30-day walk-away is in writing." |
| "Why so cheap?" | "Founding pricing. Your proof is worth more than your money right now — that honesty is also how we'll treat everything else." |
