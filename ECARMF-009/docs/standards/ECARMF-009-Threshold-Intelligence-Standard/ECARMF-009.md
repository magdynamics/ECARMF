# ECARMF-009 — Threshold Intelligence Standard

## Engineering Construction Specification

### 1. Standard Purpose

This standard establishes the mandatory construction requirements for Threshold Intelligence Standard.

### 2. Scope

This standard applies to ECARMF-compliant software platforms, services, APIs, databases, knowledge graphs, digital twins, AI agents, validation engines, and certification tooling implementing Threshold Intelligence Standard capabilities.
