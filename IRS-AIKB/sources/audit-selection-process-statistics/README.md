# IRS Audit Selection, Process, and Statistics Corpus

Retrieved from official `irs.gov` sources on 2026-07-17.

This corpus supports three separate knowledge functions:

1. Public explanations of why returns may be selected for examination.
2. IRS administrative procedures for workload selection, classification, examination, and resolution.
3. Aggregate statistics describing examination coverage and outcomes.

It must not be represented as reproducing confidential IRS selection formulas. DIF algorithms, model weights, thresholds, and internal nonpublic classification material are outside the corpus.

## Intended treatment in IRS-AIKB

- Publications and webpages: taxpayer-facing explanatory guidance.
- IRM: administrative and examination procedure, not binding legal authority.
- Data Book and NRP material: aggregate statistical and research evidence.
- LB&I campaigns: current compliance priorities, subject to continuing change monitoring.
