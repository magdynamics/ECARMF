# MAG Audit Case Operations Workspace

## Purpose

The Case Operations Workspace is the operational center connecting client input,
jurisdiction analysis, professional work, authority communications, client updates,
and verified outcomes.

## Workspace navigation

```text
Overview | Team & agents | Authorization | Evidence | Audit program | Tasks
Schedule | Authority contacts | Notices & requests | Issues & workpapers
Rights & options | Escalation | Client updates | Deliverables | Outcome
```

## External-action gate

Before a tax-authority contact, record request, document submission, protest, or
resolution request, the system validates:

- correct jurisdiction and taxpayer
- assigned human actor
- professional eligibility and approval
- accepted authorization with matching tax form and period
- applicable client decision for high-consequence actions
- privilege and evidence-release review
- verified deadline and delivery plan when applicable
- complete action logging and follow-up ownership

AI agents prepare, organize, reconcile, draft, and monitor. They do not impersonate a
person, contact the tax authority, sign an authorization, make an election, agree to an
adjustment, extend a statute, or transmit client evidence.

## Staff and AI work

Every task has an owner, reviewer, due date, source inputs, permitted actions, expected
workpaper, conclusion status, and escalation path. Staff credentials and authorization
scope are independent of application permissions.

## Customized audit programs

The program generator selects procedures from professionally reviewed jurisdiction
knowledge based on taxpayer, return, year, industry, issues, ratios, evidence, notice,
and controversy stage. The generated program remains a draft until approved. Each
procedure is assigned, performed, documented, reviewed, and concluded.

## Scheduling

The calendar distinguishes verified external deadlines, authority-promised dates, and
internal preparation dates. An external deadline requires the source document,
calculation record, professional verification, owner, filing buffer, and delivery proof.

## Client transparency

Material case events feed the client engagement layer. Released updates state what
changed, work completed, what happens next, client actions, and responsible contact.
Internal draft conclusions and privileged analysis remain controlled.
