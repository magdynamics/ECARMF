# ECARMF Vision

The vision of ECARMF is to become a practical, auditable, and extensible standard for economic capital asset risk management.

ECARMF should help decision-makers answer five core questions:

1. What is the asset?
2. What capital supports the asset?
3. What economic assumptions drive the asset?
4. What risks threaten the asset?
5. What threshold requires action?

The long-term goal is to create a framework that can be used by sponsors, lenders, regulators, auditors, investors, developers, and technology teams.
