# ECARMF-007 — Digital Twin Standard

## Engineering Construction Specification

### 1. Standard Purpose

This standard establishes the mandatory construction requirements for Digital Twin Standard.

### 2. Scope

This standard applies to ECARMF-compliant software platforms, services, APIs, databases, knowledge graphs, digital twins, AI agents, validation engines, and certification tooling implementing Digital Twin Standard capabilities.
